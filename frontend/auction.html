INTENTBRIDGE MULTI-SOLVER AUCTION
← Router

Decentralised Bid Market for Intent Execution

A sealed-bid commit–reveal auction where any solver can bid to execute a user's intent. Misbehaviour is provable on-chain via EIP-712 signatures, and slashing makes collusion economically irrational.

🔒
Trust upgrade: the original router was a single trusted off-chain process. In this design, three competing Solvers commit hashed bids; the cheapest revealed wins; and a suppressed lower bid can be proven post-hoc by anyone with a signature + matching commit hash. The misbehaving Solver loses stake. The honest challenger is paid.
